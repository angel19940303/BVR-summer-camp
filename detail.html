Middle Camp

More activities + greater choice = tremendous fun.

With over 25 different activities, this full day program is a perfect blend of structure and flexibility, giving your child the “big-kid” thrill of choosing their activities. One thing your child will find equally thrilling is meeting new friends. We purposely keep our counselor-to-camper ratio small at 1:7, to help foster a connected community where social skills are developed and everyone feels a sense of belonging. Whether it’s swimming a lap, scoring a goal or taking a bow, our counselors guide and support campers as they discover new interests, develop talents and meet challenges with confidence and enthusiasm.

Extended Day

Want more camp? You've got it. Our weekly Extended Day programs are available to all Middle Campers for an additional fee.

7:30-8:30 am - Drop off your camper up to an hour earlier than the regular drop-off at 8:30 am.

4:00-6:00 pm - Extend your camper's day as late as possible to give them full access to more activities.
