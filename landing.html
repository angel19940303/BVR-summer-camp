Extended Day

Want more camp? You've got it. Our weekly Extended Day programs are available to all Middle Campers for an additional fee.

7:30-8:30 am - Drop off your camper up to an hour earlier than the regular drop-off at 8:30 am.

4:00-6:00 pm - Extend your camper's day as late as possible to give them full access to more activities.

Transportation Options

Why not let someone else be the driver? We'll safely shuttle your camper to and from Beaver by bus.
