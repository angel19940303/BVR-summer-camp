Make it memorable.

From sun-kissed swims and fresh-air fun to outrageous art and finding new friends. Summer is short — make it memorable.

Welcome to an unforgettable summer.

Beaver Summer Camp is a day camp in Chestnut Hill for kids ages 3-15. But beyond those details, it’s a little hard to explain. Yes, it’s summer camp. But it’s also the smell of toasting marshmallows, a contagious belly laugh, and the MAGIC that happens when a group of kids is given permission to just BE. Summer goes by in the blink of an eye, so at Beaver, we make sure it’s memorable.
